How We Work

Our streamlined process ensures secure and efficient escrow management

Step 01

Contract Upload & Verification

Upload your construction contracts to our secure platform. Our system uses OCR technology to extract key terms, payment schedules, and milestone requirements, ensuring accurate setup and compliance verification.

Step 02

Escrow Account Setup

Funds are securely deposited into regulated escrow accounts with major Qatar banks. All parties receive real-time notifications and access to transparent tracking of held funds and release conditions.

Step 03

Milestone Tracking & Verification

Monitor project progress through our digital milestone tracking system. Photo evidence, digital certificates, and automated notifications ensure all parties stay informed about project status and payment readiness.

Step 04

Automated Fund Release

Once milestones are verified and approved by all parties, funds are automatically released according to the predetermined schedule. Complete audit trails and compliance documentation are maintained throughout the process.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ESQRO's escrow services

1 What is ESQRO and how does it work for construction projects?

ESQRO is a digital escrow platform specifically designed for construction projects in Qatar. We securely hold funds in regulated accounts and release payments automatically when contractual milestones are verified and approved by all parties.

2 How long does it take to set up an escrow account for my project?

Account setup typically takes 24-48 hours after contract verification. Our automated system processes documents quickly, and our team ensures compliance with all Qatar banking regulations before activation.

3 What types of construction projects can use ESQRO services?

ESQRO supports all types of construction projects including residential, commercial, infrastructure, and government contracts. Our platform is designed to handle projects from QAR 100,000 to multi-billion riyal developments.

1 How secure are the funds held in ESQRO accounts?

All funds are held in segregated accounts with Qatar Central Bank licensed institutions. We maintain bank-grade security protocols, multi-factor authentication, and complete regulatory compliance to ensure maximum protection.

2 What happens if there's a dispute during the project?

Our platform includes built-in dispute resolution mechanisms with complete audit trails. Funds remain secure until disputes are resolved through our mediation process or legal procedures as specified in the contract.

3 Is ESQRO compliant with Qatar's financial regulations?

Yes, ESQRO is fully compliant with Qatar Central Bank regulations, MOCI requirements, and all relevant financial laws. We maintain ongoing compliance monitoring and regular audits to ensure adherence to regulatory standards.

1 What kind of customer support does ESQRO provide?

We provide 24/7 customer support through multiple channels including phone, email, and live chat. Our dedicated account managers work closely with enterprise clients to ensure smooth operations and quick issue resolution.

2 Do you provide training for using the ESQRO platform?

Yes, we offer comprehensive training programs including online tutorials, live webinars, and on-site training sessions. Our team ensures all users are comfortable with the platform's features and capabilities.

3 Can ESQRO integrate with our existing project management systems?

ESQRO offers API integrations with major project management and ERP systems. Our technical team works with your IT department to ensure seamless integration and data synchronization across platforms.
